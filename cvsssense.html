CVSSense

CVSSense is a free tool that helps you calculate and understand vulnerability scores. Whether you are a security professional or just getting started, CVSSense makes the CVSS scoring process simple and accessible.

Everything runs locally on your device. No data leaves your computer, no internet connection is needed, and no account is required.
